# EstimatorAI - Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**EstimatorAI** is a standalone SaaS product that uses AI to generate accurate construction estimates in minutes. Target market: contractors, home service companies, and renovation businesses.

**Problem:** Estimating is time-consuming (2-4 hours per estimate), requires expertise, and is prone to errors.

**Solution:** AI-powered chat interface that generates detailed, itemized estimates from project descriptions and photos in under 2 minutes.

**Revenue Model:** Subscription SaaS with optional add-ons

## Pricing Strategy

### Subscription Tiers

**Free Tier**
- 5 estimates/month
- Basic features
- PDF export
- No support
- **Revenue: $0** (Lead generation)

**Pro - $49/month**
- Unlimited estimates
- Photo analysis
